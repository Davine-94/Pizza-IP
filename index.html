Our Story

Marinara Pizza Hut is a restaurant designed around the value of families in East Africa. Originally founded in 2018, the organization has grown, and is now located in Ongata Rongai Town and Langata Town. We created this restaurant because we wanted to provide authentic pizza's that offers customers a local experience.Our menu is dynamic, and our goal is to build on to the current experience of pizza restaurants in Kenya. We are guided by the principle of unity and service, and this is experienced through our in-house experience or delivery services.
Everything we do as a restaurant is a celebration of the history of pizza, and we'd love for you to experience this. The restaurant guides you through our products sourcing process to the end product, which is our pizza's.
